- The achievement of reading 13 books is hidden and appears when the reader adds a 13th book tot he reading log with message "13th Book Completed. Not always unlucky :)". The achievement then shows under achievements
- If the user presses the "Future Reads  " icon 5 times in quick succession (wihtin 3 seconds) a message appears "What does the future hold I wonder? Will Reading FC ever win the Champions League :)"
- If a user taps the enlarged avatar profile pic 3 times in quick succession a message appears below the avatar. Certain avatars have unique special messages (Sherlock, Godfather, Ronaldinho, Albert)
- If the achievement "1000 Books Completed. You Achieved it all! You are a LEDEND!" is tapped twice when enlarged in the locked icon a message appears "Is this even possible?!"
- If the Quote of the Day card is tapped 5 times in quick succession (wihtin 1.5 seconds) a secret quiz is unlocked for that day. 5 questions which change each day (cycle through 50 stored questions).
