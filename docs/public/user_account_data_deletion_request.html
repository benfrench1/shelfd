Delete Your Shelfd Account

Delete your account yourself (in-app)

  1. Navigate to the Profile screen.
  2. Select the settings cog in the top right.
  3. Select "Delete Account — Permanently remove your account and data" at the bottom.

Request account deletion by email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request deletion by emailing benfrench.r10@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account.

Once your request is verified, we will delete your account and associated personal data, including your profile information, reviews, reading log, saved books, and preferences — unless we are required to retain certain data for legal, security, or backup purposes.

We aim to process all account deletion requests within 30 days.
