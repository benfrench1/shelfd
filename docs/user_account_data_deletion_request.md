**Delete Your ShelfScore Account**

A users Shelfd account and data can be removed by the individual:

1. Navigate to the "Profile" screen
2. Select the settings cog in the top right
3. Select the option at the bottom "Delete Account - Permanently remove your account and data"

If you would like to request the deletion of your Shelfd account and associated data, please send an email to benfrench.r10@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account.

When your request is verified, we will delete your account and associated personal data, including your profile information, reviews, reading log, saved books, and preferences, unless we are required to retain certain data for legal, security, or backup purposes.

We aim to process account deletion requests within 30 days.
